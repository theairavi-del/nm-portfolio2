Concept

AI Meets Traditional Storytelling

At the start of 2023, with tools like ChatGPT and Midjourney on the rise, I saw an opportunity to bring a long time dream to life: making a comic book. I spent weeks generating thousands of images, combining them into a cohesive narrative, and used Photoshop to maintain character consistency throughout. The result was a fully realized AI-assisted visual story, blending traditional storytelling with cutting-edge technology.

Why Riso Print?

Old Tech, New Stories

I chose to Riso print this comic as a playful contrast between old and new technology. Since Cognis explores the future and AI, it felt fitting to use a retro printing aesthetic like Riso, emphasizing the blend of classic craftsmanship with futuristic themes. The irony of using older tech to print a story about cutting-edge AI gives the project a unique, nostalgic edge while staying rooted in futurism.

Process

Crafting the Visual Narrative

To match the theme of the comic, I combined all my ideas and processes to create this unique comic book. From AI-generated concepts to careful editing and Riso printing, every step was designed to bridge the gap between emerging technology and traditional art forms.
