Concept

A Revolutionary Skatepark

Sessio is a revolutionary skatepark concept that pushes the limits of what's expected. Skateboarders thrive on doing the unexpected, skating where they're not supposed to, taking risks, and standing out. That's why traditional skateparks often feel tame and uninspiring. With Sessio, I set out to redefine the skatepark by drawing inspiration from the wonders of the world, places that feel so exclusive and off-limits.

Creative Strategy

Meet Sevvy the Frog

I wanted to push the boundaries of skateboarding and make Sessio feel like a whole new world, kind of like Disney. I modeled a frog, named Sevvy, to be the mascot of Sessio. Sevvy represents that playful, rebellious spirit that skateboarding is all about, guiding riders into a realm where the rules are rewritten. The strategy was to blend wonder with a touch of the forbidden, creating an experience that turns every skate session into an epic adventure.

Advertising

Campaign

For the ad campaign, I wanted to push this idea of Sevvy traveling all over, skating the different wonders while making it look like something themepark related. I envisioned bold, vibrant visuals of Sevvy tackling iconic, off-limits skate spots that feel like epic rides, inviting skaters into a world of adventure. The campaign captures that rebellious, fun spirit of skateboarding and transforms each ride into a journey where every corner of the map is a new, exhilarating attraction.
